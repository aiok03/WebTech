Undergraduate program

Undergraduate means, a person who finished his bachelor degree, after, completes the undergraduate education; a student receives the undergraduate degree. It is the recognition of post-secondary schooling. Usually, it takes 4 years to complete the undergraduate program (bachelor degree). But the time span may vary depending on country & education field (MBBS /diploma/engineering etc.). Credit is the ultimate marking system for different courses. (Usually 3 credit a course). One student must pass all the courses & complete the total credit as well to get the degree. .
